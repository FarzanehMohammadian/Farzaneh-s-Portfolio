Resume

Hi there, my name is Farzaneh.

I have always had a dream of being a designer and had always been asked why?! I just knew that I wanted to be a designer one day, but back in that moment I did not have any answers. Now I know design is more than making things attractive. Design has influence on people. It can manipulate thoughts and feelings. That’s why I became a UX/UI designer.

For me design is so much more than creating visuals; as a UX/UI designer, I am a psychologist, researcher, and artist all at once. I know how to take a complex problem and form it into smaller and digestable pieces. I have learned to see people as real human beings not only users. I know how to listen to them and understand their goals, frustrations and pain-points and create for them a product that they love to get return to every day.

My goal is not to forget about the impact of my designs. I’m basing my design decisions on long term goals: I think beyond the users, and design not only for the time they are being used but for the time they are not being used. I spend a long time asking and researching about users’ wishes , dreams, and real needs. At the end of the day, I want my product to add value to their experience and be part of their lives.
